Poodle

Famous Poodle owners include Elvis Presley, Elizabeth Taylor, Jackie Kennedy, Lucille Ball, Marilyn Monroe, Katharine Hepburn, and Walt Disney.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Colors: Black, White, Apricot, Cream, Sable, Black & White, Blue, Grey, Silver, Brown, Red
  • Temperament: Intelligent, Alert, Active, Faithful, Instinctual, Trainable
  • Life Expectancy: 12 - 15 years

Poodles come in three sizes: Standard, Miniature, and Toy.
